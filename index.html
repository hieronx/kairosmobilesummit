Kairos Mobile Summit

Discovering the next generation of entrepreneurs ready to take the stage at the MWC 2014

It is with tremendous excitement that we announce the Kairos Mobile Summit, which will take place in Barcelona, Spain on October 18-19, 2013. This event is an opportunity to identify and accelerate the next generation of cutting-edge mobile ventures.

This meeting of the mobile minds will focus specifically on the massive potential of mobile technology to tackle big global challenges. Kairos fellows will once again join forces with expert mentors to create innovative new mobile solutions, and we will also showcase existing Kairos ventures that are already pushing the world forward in this space. The event will be hosted in the iconic Telefónica building - the Mobile World Capital Foundation HQ - in Plaça Catalunya, Barcelona.

Get ready for the Mobile Innovation Challenge, a hackathon where the best ideas from the ideation sessions will be crafted into business models with the guidance of mentors. We’ll have a Mobile Demo Day where the teams of the most innovative mobile startups will pitch their long-term plan to tackle a global challenge. Following our new partnership model, this is not simply a one-time collaboration – the top mobile ventures will earn the opportunity to attend the Mobile World Congress 2014 in Barcelona on behalf of Kairos.

See you in Barcelona!

Agenda

October 18, 2013 - Innovation Sessions

A Mobile Innovation Challenge with top-student entrepreneurs from all around the globe, big-thinkers, game changers, and innovators that will be working together to solve the worlds biggest problems. The best ideas from the ideation sessions are brought to market by students with the guidance of mentors.

October 19, 2013 - Demo Day

The founders of the most innovative mobile startups, coming out of universities world wide, will pitch their long-term plan to tackle a global challenge in front of the industry’s most powerful investors and distinguished press. The best mobile startups from the Demo Day will be awarded with a spot for the Mobile World Congress 2014.

Kairos Mobile Summit Keynotes

An exciting lineup of eminent speakers will share their latest views and address the latest developments and disruptive influences of exponentially growing mobile technologies over the course of the two days.
